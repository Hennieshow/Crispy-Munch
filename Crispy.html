The African continent has numerous and diverse cultures which have translated into its food. so we selected from the vast number to take you around the continent with these popular delicious delicacies.

Mafe, known in English as groundnut stew, is an iconic traditional African dish, popular throughout West Africa, especially of the Wolof people of the Senegal and Gambia with the ingredients and flavour varying somewhat by religion. This rich stew is thickened with ground peanuts, which gives a wonderful sweet-salty flavour. It is often made with lamb or mutton but can be made with chicken, fish or in a vegeterian version.
